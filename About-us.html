About Us

Pawan Nagar Foundation (CCT) is a registered non-governmental organization with tax-exempt status under Section 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act. CCT has been working with the marginalized and the underprivileged at Kakkanur, Odiyathur and surrounding villages of Villupuram District, Tamil Nadu since 2000. CCT has initiated programs in the field of health care, education, social and economical development to improve the living conditions of the rural communities. CCT has special focus in supporting the empowerment of the dalits and widows toward their emancipation through awareness and training programs.

Our Mission

Pawan Nagar Foundation is committed to empowering underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable development. We believe that every individual deserves equal opportunities to thrive, and we work tirelessly to make that vision a reality.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every person has access to basic needs, quality education, and the opportunity to lead a fulfilling life. Through collaboration and resource mobilization, we aim to foster a compassionate and just society.

What We Do

  • Education programs and scholarships for children and adults.
  • Healthcare initiatives including medical camps and awareness drives.
  • Community development through infrastructure and employment generation.
  • Environmental conservation and eco-friendly practice promotion.
